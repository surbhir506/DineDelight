Taste, Love, Delight

For more than two decades, Dine Delight has proudly held its place at the heart of Taj Resort's bustling tourist district. As a cherished family-owned restaurant, we've consistently blended excellence and a personal touch into every one of our culinary creations. Throughout the summer season, Dine Delight stands as the quintessential cozy dining destination, welcoming a diverse array of patrons, from tourists to locals. Whether you're seeking a sumptuous breakfast buffet experience or yearning for a taste of our delectable homemade dishes spanning breakfast, lunch, and dinner, we invite you to savor the Dine Delight difference. We eagerly await the opportunity to serve you and exceed your expectations.
